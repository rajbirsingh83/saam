Sikhs in US

Military · Technology · Medicine · Music · Fashion

  • It was almost 30 years afterwards that Simran Lamba was the first Sikh allowed to join the U.S. Army with untrimmed hair and the typical Sikh turban.

  • Narinder Singh Kapany is an Indian-born American Sikh physicist known for his work in fibre optics. He was named as one of the seven 'Unsung Heroes' by Fortune magazine in their 'Businessmen of the Century' issue (1999-11-22). He is also known as "Father of Fiber Optics".

  • Ajaypal Singh Banga had served CitiGroup for more than 13 years and had held senior management positions in the USA. In 2005 he was CEO of Citi’s international global consumer group. Since April 12, 2010, Ajay Banga is the Mastercard’s President and Chief Operating Officer.

  • Satkirin Kaur Khalsa is the most famous Sikh Kirtan singer of America. She is currently serving the Manhattan Gurdwara. She has the honor of singing the prayer for the opening ceremony of the 2009 United Nations General Assembly on Climate Change. She has performed in many countries and hosts a Kudalini Yoga show at JUS Punjabi TV.

  • Waris Ahluwalia came to the USA at the age of five and was raised in Brooklyn, New York. He is best known as a jeweler and for his small roles in Hollywood films like The Life Aquatic and Inside Man.
